Shadow of the colossus

Shadow of the colossus is an action-adventure game developed and published by Sony computer entertainment. The game takes place in a fantasy world where you play as Wander, a young man who enters an isolated and abandoned region of the realm called The Forbidden Lands in them he seeks the power to revive a girl named Mono.

In order to revive her you need to defeat sixteen massive beings, the colossi which are spread across the Forbidden Lands, but you are not alone, you travel alongside your loyal horse Agro.

I played this game for the first time with my father when i was 10 years old, I remember beating it in two days and having a lot of fun with my dad. After turning 16 my father gifted me the remaster version of the game for the PS4 and it struck me like a truck, after beating the game again after all those years I realized... That game is one of the best games i ever played.
